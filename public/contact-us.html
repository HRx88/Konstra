Work With PrintAdobe

Partner with Konstra to Deliver Industry-Led Robotics Education

PrintAdobe collaborates with selected learning centres, institutions, and education partners to deliver structured, industry-aligned training in robotics, automation, and 3D construction.

We work with partners who share our focus on quality, safety, and real-world relevance, not mass enrolment. If your organisation is interested in exploring a collaboration, we invite you to get in touch.

Who This Is For

We welcome enquiries from:

  • Robotics and STEM learning centres
  • Polytechnics and institutes of higher learning
  • Continuing education and training providers
  • Schools or programmes preparing students for STEM pathways

What Collaboration Looks Like

Partners may work with PrintAdobe to:

  • Co-deliver selected PrintAdobe training programmes
  • Offer industry-aligned progression for advanced students
  • Provide applied learning exposure beyond classroom robotics
  • Access Konstra-developed curriculum, case studies, and learning tools

All collaborations are reviewed and approved to ensure alignment with PrintAdobe standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our Customer Service Specialist is always happy to answer any queries you may have. However, in an effort to provide rapid response to you ASAP. We have created an FAQ section of the most common questions we frequently get. Kindly review the FAQ below to see if your question is answered prior to emailing us, although feel free to email us.

  1. What is Konstra?

    2. Konstra is a Singapore-based company specializing in advanced 3D printing systems for large-scale construction and architectural applications. Our machines combine robotics, automation, and precision engineering to enable on-site, free-form concrete printing.

  2. What makes Konstra's 3D printing systems unique?

    3. Our platforms are designed for mobility, adaptability, and strength. They feature custom track systems, hydraulic stabilization, and multi-axis robotic arms that allow printing in complex geometries and challenging terrains.

  3. What materials can the Konstra printers use?

    4. Konstra machines are compatible with a wide range of concrete and mortar formulations, including industrial, architectural, and specialty mixes tailored for various environmental and structural requirements.

  4. Where can Konstra systems be deployed?

    5. Konstra's mobile platforms are engineered for use across diverse sites — from urban developments to remote, uneven terrains. Hydraulic outriggers and extendable track systems ensure stable operation on different ground conditions.

  5. What is the difference between the K1 and K2 models?

    6. The K1 is Konstra's current working flagship model — a fully operational, track-mounted 3D printing platform optimized for high stability and precision. The K2 is an upcoming system, currently in development, focused on improved portability, integrated automation, and enhanced material handling.

  6. How does the system ensure print accuracy?

    7. Our machines utilize multi-axis robotic control with advanced motion calibration, ensuring consistent print precision even during large-scale or multi-directional printing operations.

  7. Does Konstra provide training or support?

    8. Yes. Konstra offers full operational training, maintenance guidance, and technical support o ensure our partners can deploy and manage the system effectively on-site.

  8. Is Konstra's technology sustainable?

    9. Sustainability is central to our mission. By enabling local, on-site production with optimized material usage, our systems reduce transportation costs, waste, and carbon emissions associated with traditional construction.
