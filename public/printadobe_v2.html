The Vision

PrintAdobe is Konstra's training program built to teach students and residents the core skills needed in 3D-printed construction. The program is delivered through online workshops and in-person field training.

Students follow structured online modules through the Print Adobe dashboard, and top performers are offered a chance to join our Phnom Penh redevelopment sites to assist in real 3D-printing builds.

The Why

At Konstra, we believe that construction is more than just building structures — it's about building futures. Too many communities across Southeast Asia face the challenge of unstable housing and limited access to safe infrastructure. PrintAdobe exists to change that.

Through knowledge, technology, and collaboration, we're empowering students, residents, and builders to take part in shaping their own environments. Every print, every wall, and every design choice represents progress — the kind that gives people dignity, safety, and a sense of belonging.
