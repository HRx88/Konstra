Model K1

Description

The Konstra system is a mobile 3D-printing platform engineered for construction in demanding environments. Built on a custom track-mounted base, it offers hydraulic outriggers for up to one-tonne load stability and a Japanese diesel engine for dependable on-site power.

Its 270-degree rotational range and extendable tracks provide superior reach and balance across rough terrain. A multi-axis KUKA robotic arm enables complex, free-form concrete printing, while compatibility with diverse mortar and concrete formulations supports industrial, architectural, and experimental applications.

Product Features

  • Custom platform with integrated tracks for stable and versatile mobility
  • Hydraulic outriggers on all four corners, each capable of carrying up to 1 tonne for enhanced site stability
  • Japanese diesel engine for reliable, efficient power supply in remote or rugged environments
  • Remote rough terrain mobility enabling deployment across challenging and uneven construction sites
  • 270-degree rotational freedom beyond the main machine mount, allowing highly flexible printing orientations
  • Extendable track legs (+0.5 meters on each side) to increase footprint and operational stability when required
  • Multi-axis robotic arm (KUKA) for complex free-form 3D printing and adaptive project scaling
  • Compatible with a wide range of concrete and mortar formulations, supporting industrial, architectural, and specialty applications
  • Modular rail system (work in progress) to allow scalable, continuous printing across larger project footprints
