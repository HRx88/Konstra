HARNESSING THE FUTURE OF CONSTRUCTION THE KONSTRA WAY.

PrintAdobe is Konstra’s education-driven initiative designed to train and inspire students and members of the public, the core skills needed in 3D-printed construction.

Our hands-on workshops provide participants with practical experience in operating 3D printing equipment, understanding the principles of 3D printing, and exploring the possibilities of sustainable construction. Alternaitvely, participants can look forward to our overseas immersion trip to Cambodia.

Why Konstra

Reducing Waste

One Layer At a Time

Konstra uses automated 3D-printing precision to place material only where it’s needed. Less waste, lower emissions, and a cleaner path to sustainable construction.

Maximum Efficiency

Minimal Effort

Konstra uses automated 3D-printing precision to build faster with fewer delays and less rework. Shorter timelines, tighter costs, and consistent quality from start to finish.

Limitless Potential

Deployable Anywhere

Konstra’s modular 3D-printing systems are designed to deploy in places traditional construction struggles to reach. Faster setup, less logistics overhead, and dependable builds across different terrains and environments.

Supporting Crews

Not a Replacement

Konstra is built to support people on-site, not replace them. Our systems take on repetitive and high-risk work so crews can focus on craftsmanship, coordination, and quality.

PROJECT STRAVA

BRINGING THE FUTURE OF CONSTRUCTION TO THOSE WHO NEEDS IT MOST.

Every person deserves the essentials of life - a safe home, stability, and the ability to call a place home.

Project Cambodia 2026 is dedicated to building that foundation through innovation in construction. By uniting advanced 3D printing technology with sustainable design, we aim to create homes that restore comfort, security, and hope - ensuring no one is left without the structures that sustain life.
