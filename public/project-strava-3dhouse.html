Description

A durable 3D-printed studio designed for affordability and practicality. This layout offers a multifunctional open interior, natural cross-ventilation, and insulated wall segments to reduce heat. Ideal for single residents transitioning from informal housing. The compact footprint allows for higher density, making it suitable for community clusters in Point A or Point B.
