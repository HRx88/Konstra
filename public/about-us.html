WHO IS KONSTRA?

Konstra was born from a passion for transforming traditional building methods into something extraordinary.

Founded by experienced architects and builders, we saw the potential of 3D printing to revolutionize how we create and experience architecture. Starting with small, ambitious projects, we partnered with leading robotic and engineering experts to grow our capabilities.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of the 3D printing construction movement, committed to making high-quality design accessible and sustainable for a better future.

FINDING INSPIRATION IN EVERY LAYER

Konstra is a pioneer in 3D printing construction, dedicated to redefining the building industry through advanced additive manufacturing. With a blend of innovative design, expert craftsmanship, and sustainable practices, we aim to create structures that are both beautiful and functional. Our team combines extensive experience in architecture, engineering, and robotics, allowing us to push the boundaries of what's possible in construction today.

MEET OUR FOUNDER

Our founder, Kaneshka Salehi has been involved with countless housing and restaurant projects in the United States. And it has always been a partnership between Singapore and the United States, California for joint design collaboration and product innovation. For the past 18 months we have been innovating around housing development projects that utilise 3D printing technology to construct unique, affordable and sustainable homes. The business aims to address the housing crisis by providing a fast, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly solution. Our team has extensive experience in Architecture, Construction and Sustainable design, and we believe that using 3D printing for relevant projects can make a significant impact in the World.
